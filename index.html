How to Use ✋

SignBridge detects ASL hand gestures in real-time using your webcam and converts them to text. Currently supports 6 gestures:

✊
A
Fist, thumb to side
🖐
B
4 fingers up, thumb tucked
🤏
C
Curved hand, C shape
👆
L
Index up + thumb out
🤙
Y
Thumb + pinky out
🖖
W
3 fingers up
